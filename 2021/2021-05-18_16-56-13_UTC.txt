KAYPAKKAYA KOBANE CONTINUES AND WARS (TKP/ML - TIKKO)

Pictured on the banner is İbrahim Kaypakkaya, a Turkish revolutionary who was arrested in the aftermath of the 1971 military memorandum. He was tortured for a quarter of a year, before being executed.

Today he is remembered and revered by many around the world, as a symbol of resistance and the powerful mind of the revolutionaries.
