Newspaper clipping requesting prayers from "all true Christians" for Caption John Brown, a radical abolitionist and religious fanatic who during the civil war lead many raids in order to free slaves from plantations, as he had concluded that violent action was going to the be the only way for them to be freed, after many unsuccessful years preaching for it.

After his capture Henry David Thoreau wrote a speech entitled "A Plea for Captain John Brown" that was eventually published.
