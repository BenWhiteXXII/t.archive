"An American F-105 warplane is shot down as the pilot ejects and opens his parachute," taken by North Vietnamese photograper Mai Nam. September 1966, near Vinh Phuc, north of Hanoi. 

The pilot of the aircraft was taken hostage and held in a Hanoi prison from 1966 to 1973.
