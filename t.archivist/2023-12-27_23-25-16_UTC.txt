Paintings of Hyperborea/ Ancient Russia/ Russia before the invention of alcohol by Ivanov Vsevolod Borisovich.

"Ivanov Vsevolod Borisovich was born on August 14, 1950 in the city of Belomorsk. He started as an amateur painter. In 1978 he graduated from the Tver Art School named after A.G. Venetsianova, having received the specialty of artist-designer. Later he worked by profession and was engaged in graphics. Participant of domestic and foreign exhibitions. In his work, his focus is Russia thousands of years ago. "
