Deliblatska Peščara (Deliblato Sands), the largest sandy terrain in Europe.
It is a remnant of a prehistoric desert that was formed following the withdrawal of the Pannonian Sea, with a unique elliptical shape created during the Ice age.
It is home to many endemic species of plants and animals which are rare or endangered in Europe and globally, 900 diverse plant and 200 animal species.
The Deliblato is an expanse of Aeolian processes with significant dune-like  relief made of powerful sandy deposits. It is a unique feature in this  part of the world as the only exemplar of the true sandy relief. Dunes  are 70 to 200 m (230 to 660 ft) tall.
