Smokespiration: Maki Asakawa ( 浅川マキ)(1942-2010), legendary Japanese blues and folk singer, she was initially directed to become an enka singer, but was "discovered" by Shuji Terayama who instead pushed her towards blues and helped her hone her skills in singing and performing. She released over 30 albums in her career and was highly regarded for her live concerts as well as her collaborations with free jazz pianist Yosuke Yamashita and world famous composer/songwriter Ryuchi Sakamoto.
