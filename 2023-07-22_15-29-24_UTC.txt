Andy Goldsworthy is a sculptor, environmentalist, and artist from the UK. Referring to what he creates as "land art," using only naturally found materials from his environment to create pieces that are meant to fall apart and disappear. Having spent a lot of his life as a farm laborer, he has likened The patient and painstaking proces to create his sculptures as "like planting potatoes, you just need to get into the rhythm of it."
