Fr John J. Brown, the first full-blooded Blackfoot Indian to become a priest of the Roman Catholic church, bestows blessings upon (left to right) Joe Woodcock, Chief Paul Charlo, and Pete Beaverhead in historic St. Ignatius Mission here. Brown, whose native name is Ksistaki-Poka, was ordained a short while before this picture was taken. 
Missoula, Montana, 6/21/1948.
