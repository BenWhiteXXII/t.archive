Guns and ammunition found within Ted Kaczinsky's (AKA, the Unabomber) cabin home in Montana.

Shown in order- .22 caliber rifle, Remington Model 700, MP-25 pistol, a homemade handgun, Remington .30-60 ammunition wrapped in foils, peanut butter jar with ammunition.

Among the guns and ammunition there were also find an unmarked bomb and products to make more.
