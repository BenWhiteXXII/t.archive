Malcom X peeks out his window with a M1 carabine after receiving death threats, 14 February 1965. 
These threats where serious and days later on 21 February 1965 he got killed. Malcom X was addressing the Organization of Afro-American Unity in Manhattan's Audubon Ballroom when someone ran up to him and hit him in the chest with a sawed-off shotgun and two other men ran up to the stage firing semi-automatic handguns. Malcom X was pronounced dead at 3:30 pm after arriving in the hospital. He got hit 21 times including 10 buckshot hits. The 3 people who shot him where identified as members of the Nation of Islam, Malcom X was a part of that organization till 8 March 1964.
